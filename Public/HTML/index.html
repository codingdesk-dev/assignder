The Faculty of Technology at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura is a top-tier institution that provides high-quality education in technology. With a focus on practical, hands-on learning and cutting-edge research, the faculty prepares students for successful careers in a range of technology-related fields. The faculty offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in areas such as civil, electrical and electronic engineering, information technology. The Faculty of Technology is committed to excellence in education and research, and is dedicated to preparing the next generation of technology leaders.

Alpha CodeNox is a software development project team created by a group of students from the Department of Information Technology at the Faculty of Technology, University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Developed as part of our Fundamentals of Programming course, the project showcases the group's skills in software development, programming, and problem-solving. With a focus on user experience and functionality, Alpha CodeNox is a testament to the group's dedication and hard work, and represents their commitment to excellence in technology education.
